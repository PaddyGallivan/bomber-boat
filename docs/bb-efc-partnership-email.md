<p style="color:#C8102E;font-weight:bold;font-size:15px;margin-bottom:4px;">ONE MORE THOUGHT &#8212; SIMON MADDEN</p>

<p>378 games. Four premierships. A Norm Smith Medal. 300 good games, 78 great ones. One of the greatest to ever pull on the red and black &#8212; and by all accounts a storyteller who will keep going long after the sailors have gone to Marvel and come back for the return voyage down the Maribyrnong.</p>

<p>There is also a scoreboard named after him at St Bernard&#39;s College &#8212; though there is genuine debate within the college walls about whether it might be for one of his sons. Josh, a Wall of Fame VAFA footballer and regular State Representative, or Isaac, a dangerous small forward who kicked goals for the Saints and did his selection chances no harm whatsoever. Debatable. Impressive either way.</p>

<p>And his brother Justin played 45 games for Essendon before heading to Carlton for another 287. We will not hold that against the family. Between them the Madden brothers gave 710 games to this competition &#8212; Simon loyal to the red and black for every single one of his.</p>

<p>Simon Madden on the water, telling stories on the way to the game &#8212; that is content the club and the media would love.</p>
