## Posting tips

- **Pin** the post in groups where you can.

- **Image**: attach the poster (`marketing/round8-poster.html` printed to PDF, or the IG post image `bomberboat-post.png` from `assets/brand/make_images.py`).

- **Engagement**: reply to every comment in the first 2 hours.
